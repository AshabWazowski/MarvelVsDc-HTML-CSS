Superman

Superman or Kal-L is the most strongest superhero in the entire DC Comics. He has immense strength compared to any other superhero. An alien to earth yet he managed to conceal himself by making a secret identity of Clark Kent, a reporter for Daily Planet. He is the leader of Justice League alongside Batman. The duo of Superman & Batman is considered as the most powerful duo in the entire comics history. Superman is immune to almost everything made by manwhether it's a bullet or a missile or even a nuclear bomb however he has just one weakness revealed till now, i.e., Kryptonite, a kind of stone from the planet Krypton where he was born. Due to his immense power and impenetrable skin he is considered as a God among humans.
