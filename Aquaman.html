AQUAMAN

Arthur Curry or Aquaman is the King of the Seven Seas. Just like Superman & Wonder Woman he has also got a long list of powers, but the most significant among them is his telepathic ability to communicate with marine life which he can summon from great distances. He even possesses the Trident of Poseiden, an unbreakable three pronged leister which is known to have God's own power.
