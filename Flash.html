The Flash

By far there have been 4 characters who have assumed the mantle of Flash. The most popular among them is Barry Allen. Flash is known for his superhuman speed. He can run with the speed of light & is also known to have reversed timelines. He stores his costume of Flash in a special ring via the use of some special gas. He is among the young members of Justice League. He is so fast than he can even run on water without drowning and is known to have gone back in time several times. He even has visited parallel universe with his speed. He is even fast in thinking & also possess superhuman reflexes. That's why he is known as the fastest man alive.
